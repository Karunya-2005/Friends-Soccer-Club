The club

Football, coached with patience. Community, built on purpose.

Friends Soccer Academy started with eleven neighbourhood kids and one borrowed pitch. Today we run six squads across three grounds, but the idea hasn't changed: every player gets real coaching, real minutes, and a team that knows their name.

We're a not-for-profit run by parents, volunteer coaches, and a few players who never left. Fees stay low, kit is shared where it's needed, and nobody sits out a season for lack of funds.
